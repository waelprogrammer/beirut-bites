Our Story

Born from the Heart
of Beirut

Beirut Bites was born in 2009 when Nadia and Karim Khalil left their homeland carrying nothing but their grandmother's recipe notebook and an unshakeable passion for sharing Lebanese culture through food.

What began as a small corner café has grown into a beloved destination, yet our soul has never changed. Every morning we hand-roll our pastries, grind our own spice blends, and source our olive oil from the same family grove in northern Lebanon.

We believe that great Lebanese food is an act of hospitality — a warm welcome to our table and to a culture rich in flavor, history, and generosity.
